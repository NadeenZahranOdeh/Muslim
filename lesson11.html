Invalidators of Prayer

The Definition

In Islam, prayer (Salah) is invalidated by certain actions that violate its physical purity, prescribed posture, or intent. Core nullifiers include breaking ritual purity (Wudu/Ghusl), deliberate speech, eating or drinking, excessive unnecessary movement, and intentionally turning away from the Qiblah direction.

Core Invalidators of Prayer:

1-Loss of Purity. 2-Intentional Speech.3-Eating and Drinking.4- Excessive Movement.5-Turning Away from the Qiblah.6- Uncovering the Awrah.7-Intentionally Changing Intention.8-Omitting an Integral (Rukn).9-Laughing.
