What is the ruling on abandoning prayer?

The Definition

This covers the Islamic legal status (Hukm) concerning a person who leaves prayer, detailing the distinction between intentional abandonment out of denial versus laziness, and the scholarly consensus regarding its gravity.

Why learn this level

This knowledge serves as a necessary warning to protect one's faith (Iman), emphasizing the danger of neglecting this essential bond and the importance of prioritizing prayer above worldly concerns.
